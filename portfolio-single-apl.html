Athletic Propulsion Labs

Advertising Campaign

Project Details

OVERVIEW

APL became known in the industry when they became the first apparel brand that was banned from the NBA. They were banned when testing showed that the technology inside their shoes instantly improved a persons jump height. They now use this technology inside basketball shoes, as well as secondary markets like tennis and running. Being in a higher price bracket than most shoes, they are a premium brand that know there game improving potential is a priceless asset.


PROBLEM

Since they were banned in 2008, they have steadily decreased in sales and profits. The excitement around the brand has diminished, even though their ability to enhance your game has only improved.


SOLUTION

Considering the brand already had such a powerful benefit for the consumer, a focus on re-igniting excitement around the brands ability to elevate a persons game was the main focus for the campaign. This was dramatized and supported with visuals supporting the sensation and views that can only be achieved by flight. The campaign enforced that APL is the only choice if you want to push your game to the next level.
