Project Details

OVERVIEW

A new Identity system was developed to solidify Howard’s as a foundation in the NW dining scene. Cabin environments were a source of inspiration as they united the feel of a cozy and inviting environment, while staying true to Howard’s overall mindset and culture. A menu system and nutrition guide were immediately introduced to help solidify this new identity system.
