About Me

My experience with design began 4 years ago as a freelance designer. I was given great opportunities to work across both web and print design during that time period. All in all, I had the chance to work with over 40 clients, such as E2 Entertainment, eBay, Mahngo and Inkykiss Communications to name a few. Currently, I am the graphic designer at Benchmade Knife Company. My role there includes a lot, but I usually find myself supporting our website with new graphics and quick code changes, creating new campaign collateral in both digital and print formats, as well as product and lifestyle photography. The majority of my experience involves designing for print - anything from business cards, letterheads, brochures, large format banners and signage and tradeshow graphics. I also work across a wide range of digital mediums, from web advertisements and graphics, .gif animations, all the way to getting my fingers dirty with some HTML / CSS code.
