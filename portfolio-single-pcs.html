Portland Center Stage

Print Collateral

Project Details

OVERVIEW

Portland Center Stage is a theatre located in Portland, Oregon’s Pearl District. Almost exclusively hosting live performances, the theatre has a strong following for fans of story telling and live acting. Each year, the location hosts an average of 13 different plays, typically spanning for a month at a time.


PROBLEM

This year, a collaboration with This American Life brought several new plays to Portland Center Stages line up. Each needed unique artwork that related to the story. This artwork would be used on a wide variety of collateral pieces, so modularity is an important requirement.
“The Middle of Nowhere” is a story about Nauru, an extremely small island in the Atlantic Octean, that after seeing human colonization in the 1800’s, became a center point for a variety of unfortunate, illegal and unethical activities.


SOLUTION

To be the most effective at far away distances , complementary color was used to catch a viewers attention. Initially based off of a hand sketch, the drawing was scanned and redrawn in vector format. A strong feeling of isolation and discomfort with extra weight on the left side of the poster all add to the story of Nairu’s scarred history.
