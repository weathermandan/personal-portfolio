Project Yellow

Advertising Campaign & Collateral

Project Details

OVERVIEW

Project Yellow is a safety initiative for the Portland Metro Area. The main goal is to share safety information and lower safety related accidents in the area during the process.


PROBLEM

Although Portland is one of the safest cities in the US when it comes to roadway safety, the city still had high ambitions for creating safer streets. PBOT aims to make our transportation system the safest possible and to move toward zero traffic related deaths within the next 10 years.Sharing key safety information and an overall message to be more aware were the main focuses of the campaign.


SOLUTION

In order to make the campaign as successful as possible, the target audience was focused on the most statistically dangerous drivers on the road, 16-19 year old teenage men. As opposed to the way typical safety initiatives target teenage audiences, Project Yellow focused on supporting and rallying the target by calling them out. Showing them the statistics, and showing them how they can avoid becoming one.

An identity system was established for the campaign, as well as a series of printed and digital mediums. An app was also proposed as a way for users to see accident prone areas of the city and share this information on social media.
