Neo Assist

Web Design and Collateral

Project Details

OVERVIEW

Neo Assist is a customer support company based out of Sau Paulo, Brazil. They supply companies with a completely self automated chat support bot, as well as a series of customer relations tools. Neo Assist wanted their website to more accurately reflect the tech side of their business, while also delivering a better User Experience.


PROBLEM

Neo Assist began offering more complex and intelligent services for their customer base, and needed a website that reflected their sophisticated use of technology.

Neo Assist also began hosting annual customer support expoâ€™s in Brazil (called Nexus) and needed appropriately designed invitations to showcase how important this years event would be.

The client had a newly established logo, but no examples or guidance existed for how to show the new brand.


SOLUTION

A system of shapes were developed based heavily off the cloud logo that the client had just established as the new company logo. The friendly and approachable nature of the logo led to a more established look for the brand. Custom made icons and subtle complexities in the use of patterns all kept these principles in mind, leading the way for the future of a cohesive brand experience for the clients customers.
