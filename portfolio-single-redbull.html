Redbull

Advertising & Web Design

Project Details

OVERVIEW

Redbull has been taking strides to reduce their carbon footprint. All cans are 100% recyclable: once they've been collected and reached the recycling facilities, not the tiniest bit of aluminium is lost. They can be melted down and reused an infinite number of times with absolutely no loss in quality. Using reclaimed aluminium comprises a 95% saving in energy compared to using new aluminium. Today, Red Bull cans weigh significantly less than some years ago. The constant decrease in weight also makes a difference: the less aluminium goes into cans in the first place, the smaller the carbon footprint.


PROBLEM

Redbull needs to share their message with their customer base. People feel empowered when they choose products that support and acknoledge issues that they find important. And while Redbull clearly isn't making these changes for profit, their is currently no strong messaging sharing the work they've done, and the difference it has made.


SOLUTION

To support communication between the brand its users, a micro site was developed purely focusing on the story of Redbull's recycling mission. Hand drawn illustrations related to Redbulls target and stayed on brand, while halftone graphics were introduced to create a clear distinction between existing branded collateral. Posters and billboards were also developed to further support the message.
